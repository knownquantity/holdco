ninety nine labs

crypto-native product studio connecting people and ideas in unexpected ways

built by people, for people

drops

  • capture the bag is a memetic game where players compete to capture and hold a digital pot of sol
  • ctrl+enter is a browser-native curatorial primitive for beta testing the internet
  • handoff is an internet experiment testing the strength of word-of-mouth distribution mechanics and intention-driven rituals
  • mixed messages is a collective emotional api for navigating the messiness of being alive, online and in between

lore

  • before the internet became a stage, it was a workshop
  • then, it got optimized. content turned into performance. social into scale. culture into collateral.
  • what was once experiential and exclusive is now designed to be widely documented, distributed and consumed
  • ninetynine labs is a return to the workshop era. where things are made deliberately, together, and behind closed doors
  • networks, social experiences and community products optimized for intimacy and intrigue
  • the most valuable online destinations will feel secret, and the best parties ungoogleable
  • culture scales sideways, not up
  • shared rituals and cultural capital are better building blocks for social graphs
  • symbolic access and earned intimacy will define the next breakout consumer applications
  • 1999 was a fun year
  • when done deliberately, human-to-human interaction becomes a powerful engine for discovery, creativity and belonging
  • when done with intention, humans can move ideas and culture further than a feed ever could
