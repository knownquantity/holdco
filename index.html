ninetynine labs,

a crypto-native creative studio connecting people and ideas in unexpected ways

before the internet became a stage, it was a workshop

a place for experiments, not performances. for connection, not scale

now, everything is optimized - content as performance, culture as collateral. what was once exclusive and experiential is now designed to be widely documented, distributed and consumed

ninetynine labs is a return to the workshop era

we build networks, social experiences and communities designed for intimacy and intrigue

online and offline destinations that feel secret, where culture moves sideways, not up

we believe the next wave of consumer applications will be built on:

  • symbolic access and earned intimacy
  • shared rituals and cultural capital as the foundation of social graphs
  • human-to-human interaction as the core driver of discovery, creativity and belonging - not feeds or algorithms
